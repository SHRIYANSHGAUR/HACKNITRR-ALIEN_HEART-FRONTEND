No-touch Controls

  • Category: Computer-VISION Application
  • Project date: 01 March, 2022
  • Technology: Python/Flask/OpenCv/Mediapipe/numpy

A python script that lets you control your favourite Games via Hand Gestures

The app starts a web cam which captures the movement of players and accordingly controls the game without a single touch!
This is the future of gaming where hardware gaming console will be long forgotten as we step into modern era of no-touch gaming!
The app requires minimum 2GB RAM as the it created tensorflow-lite delegate on system while running.
